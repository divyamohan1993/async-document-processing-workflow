Annual Technology Report 2024

Executive Summary
This report provides a comprehensive overview of technology initiatives undertaken during the fiscal year 2024. Key achievements include the successful migration to cloud infrastructure, implementation of advanced security protocols, and the launch of three new customer-facing applications.

Key Findings
1. Cloud Migration: Completed migration of 95% of on-premise infrastructure to AWS, resulting in a 40% reduction in operational costs.
2. Security Enhancements: Implemented zero-trust architecture across all critical systems, reducing security incidents by 60%.
3. Application Development: Successfully launched mobile banking app, customer portal, and internal workflow management system.
4. Team Growth: Engineering team expanded from 45 to 72 members, with a focus on full-stack and DevOps expertise.

Recommendations
- Continue investment in cloud-native technologies
- Expand AI/ML capabilities for predictive analytics
- Strengthen cybersecurity training programs
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and observability
